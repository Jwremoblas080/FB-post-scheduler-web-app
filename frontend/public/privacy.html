Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 15, 2026

1. Introduction

FB Post Scheduler ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Facebook post scheduling service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our service, we collect:

  • Facebook Account Information: Your Facebook user ID and name (obtained through Facebook Login)
  • Facebook Page Information: List of Facebook Pages you manage and their access tokens
  • Post Content: Text, images, and videos you upload to schedule for posting
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our service (scheduled posts, posting times)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your identity via Facebook Login
  • Access your Facebook Pages to schedule and publish posts on your behalf
  • Store your scheduled posts until they are published
  • Provide and improve our service

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data (access tokens) is encrypted at rest
  • AWS Infrastructure: Data is stored on secure AWS servers (DynamoDB, S3)
  • HTTPS: All data transmission uses secure HTTPS connections
  • Access Control: Only you can access your scheduled posts

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for purposes other than providing our service

We only share data with:

  • Facebook: To publish posts on your behalf (as authorized by you)
  • AWS: Our hosting provider (under strict data processing agreements)

6. Facebook Permissions

Our app requests the following Facebook permissions:

  • pages_show_list: To display your Facebook Pages
  • pages_read_engagement: To read post engagement data
  • pages_manage_posts: To create and publish posts on your Pages

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites.

7. Data Retention

  • Scheduled Posts: Deleted automatically after successful publication
  • Failed Posts: Retained for 30 days for retry purposes
  • Account Data: Deleted when you disconnect your Facebook account

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we store about you
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data by disconnecting your account
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect our app from your Facebook account at any time

9. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Authentication (JWT tokens stored in localStorage)
  • User preferences (theme settings)

We do NOT use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your rights, contact us at:

Email: privacy@yourapp.com

13. Compliance

This service complies with:

  • Facebook Platform Policy
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
